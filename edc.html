    ABOUT US

    VISION
    Entrepreneurship in India is yet to reveal its hidden potential.It will not only help India to frontier with the world leaders but also unlock the quality of brains that we are so proud of. With this idea, The Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIT Delhi wishes to inculcate and Enrich the entrepreneurial environment in India by creating an easily accessible and exhaustive set of resources for the entrepreneurs, which including the students, the budding professionals, mentors, angel investors and the venture capitalists through various fun-filledyet educating sessions such as Startup Showcases, competitions, eTalks and so on and so forth.
    MISSION
    To create a sustainable and diverse entrepreneurial ecosystem where the young and the elderly come together to take entrepreneurship a step ahead in India.

    EVENTS


    ONE DAY CEO CHALLENGE

    Form your own teams of six and go head to head against other teams in this three-tier competition. Each level, more grueling than the last, made to test every aspect from critical thinking to being able to socialize and connect. Never-Before-Organised, this event, puts you to test, which end up with unparalleled rewards, in addition to the memories and accolades.


    INVESTOR'S PITCH

    Already have an exciting idea for a startup but looking for funding? eDC IIT Delhi presents to you “Investor’s Pitch” - a one-of-its-kind exclusive pitching event, which would be held during BECon. Be a part of this unique event and get a chance to win funding worth Rs. 2.5 million. All the selected participants would get around 8-10 minutes to prove their mettle and bag funding worth Rs. 2.5 Million. So, get ready to showcase your skills and take your startup idea to a whole new level. Mark your calendars to witness an exclusive event as innovative minds compete to turn their ideas into reality.

    CAREER FAIR

    The Business and Entrepreneurship Conclave (BECon) - flagship E-Summit of eDC IIT Delhi and is the biggest business and Entrepreneurship event in IIT presents Career Fair, a targeted event for startups to meet with potential candidates exploring full-time jobs and internship opportunities. The Career fair is an opportunity for startups, companies, VCs, government agencies to interview job seekers in one location. Meet highly skilled participants of BECon in an informal setting and hire students from diverse universities and backgrounds.


    ONE DAY INTERNSHIP

    One Day internship where selected participants will spend a day in the company to go beyond talks and learn from actual implementation. Participants will get a chance for afull day tour ofoffice premises, engage in a Live Problem and/or Brainstorming session, facilitate interaction with employees and work on problems in groups, certificates from the company for satisfactory/meritorious performance.

     STARTUP EXPO

    "Startup Expo", the flagship event of eDC IIT Delhi, which aims to bring together the most happening and innovative startups, along with C-level executives from the top corporations, government agencies, incubator investors, HNWIS, VC funds, banks, students and technology companies. This event would serve as a perfect stage for all the startups to present themselves in front of a diverse audience. The daylong event will feature thought leaders, experienced entrepreneurs and investors taking part in keynotes, panel discussions and interactive sessions. So get ready to witness an amazing showcase of talent as we invite innovative product based startups to exhibit their mettle to the top mentors and investors from India and abroad at BECON IIT Delhi.


    CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

    Passionate about entrepreneurship? eDC IIT Delhi is back with its exciting Campus Ambassador Program. We are looking for enthusiastic individuals with a zeal to promote the promising world of entrepreneurship among the young aspiring minds. If the challenging and amazingly rewarding nature of entrepreneurship excites your heart, this program offers you a great opportunity to associate with IIT Delhi- an institute which has produced some of the greatest and most innovative entrepreneurs of India.
